Welcome!

In this classic game, you'll select rock, paper, or scissors to try to beat the computer. Remember:

  • Rock crushes scissors
  • Paper covers rock
  • Scissors cuts paper

If your guess beats the computer, you get a point. If the computer beats you, it gets a point. If there's a tie, no one gets a point.

First one to 5 points wins.
